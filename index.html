Project II: Air Quality in Salt Lake City

Abstract: In this data analytics project, I investigate how Salt Lake City’s air quality has changed over time by using python to read 38 years of csv files from a government website into pandas dataframes, then using matplotlib to identify any trends visually. After identifying the two primary pollutants causing poor air quality in the valley, I discuss how we might be able to reduce them.
